Privacy Policy — Solar Setup Studio

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Solar Setup Studio ("the App") is developed and operated by Jasher Altenor. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it's used.

What we collect

The App collects your email address only if you choose to enter it to unlock full system sizing results in the Custom Builder. This is the only personal information the App collects.

All other data — your appliance list, calculated loads, saved system designs, and builder inputs — stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

How we use your email address

If you provide your email address, we use it to:

  • Send you information about the Solar Off-Grid Course and related content
  • Occasionally follow up about solar/off-grid planning resources

We do not sell your email address to third parties.

Data sharing

Your email address may be shared with email delivery or marketing automation services solely to send the communications described above.

Data retention

We retain your email address until you ask us to delete it or unsubscribe.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete your email address at any time by contacting us at jasher_altenor@hotmail.com.

Tracking

The App does not use advertising identifiers and does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
