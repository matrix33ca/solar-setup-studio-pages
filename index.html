Frequently asked

Are the sizing numbers exact?
No — everything in the app is a planning estimate. Always confirm your final system design with a qualified solar installer or electrician before purchasing equipment.

Why do I need to enter my email in the Builder?
Unlocking your full inverter/battery/solar recommendation is free — we just ask for an email so we can follow up with more off-grid planning resources, including the Solar Off-Grid Course.

Is my data private?
Your appliance list and saved designs stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
