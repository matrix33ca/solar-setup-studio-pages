Common questions

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. There's no account and nothing syncs to a server.

How do I add a task by voice? Tap the microphone icon next to the task input bar and speak — it fills in automatically and submits after a short pause.

How do routines work? Mark a task as a routine and give it a name (like "Morning Routine") in its editor — it repeats daily and groups with other tasks under the same name in the dome's task list.
